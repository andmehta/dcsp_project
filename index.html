Always the Best Fish

Our top priority is making sure that the fish we trade are heatlhy and responsibly sourced. One of the best parts of our community is that it allows for third-part vendors, such as yourself, to sell from their own aquariums. This allows for Nemo's bowl to connect acquirium ownders worldwide.!

One of the most dangerous partSs of working with aquariums is the movement from animals from one tank to another. We realize this and want to ensure our users that your fish will always been transported safely... it is for this reason that we use top of the line packaging and mandatory 2 day shipping.

Plants of Promise

Once you decide on the species you want in your aquarium, we want to make sure that those fish have appropiate plant life in their tank. We worked with marine biologist to create a recommended list of plants for various species and species combinations. Also, having the right plants isn't just good for the fist.... good plants can improve any tank!

Again, we take the enviroment seriously and recognize the negative impact that corporations have had with irresponsible sourcing. To combate this we not only pledge to exclusively source from sustainlable fisheries, but we are also willing to take another step. With the Plants of Promise program, each time you buy a plant species we will match you by planting the same species in a damaged ecosystem!
